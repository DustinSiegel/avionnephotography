## 🚀 Best Practices:

1. **Vary Your Approach**
   - Use "None" for first few follow-ups
   - Add service offers as you expand capabilities

2. **Match Energy to Agent**
   - Casual agents → Fun, emoji-heavy intros
   - Professional agents → Data-driven, value-focused snippets

3. **Track What Works**
   - Note which combinations get responses
   - Adjust your selection patterns

4. **Stay Authentic**
   - Only use service offers when you actually have that capability
   - The excitement in the snippets should match your real feelings

5. **Timing Matters**
   - Use "just got" snippets when equipment is truly new
   - Switch to "now offering" after a few months
